HG Beginning.

A well thought and planned dream brought about HG. None of the resources we needed to actualize our dream of High Fashion gave us the opportunity and autonomy we needed to succeed. So We built our own. And HG was born. Today, HG provides services to both the highly tasteful customers and the people just starting out in High Fashion.

HG Mission

Making High Fashion for Everyone.
We aim to help people around the world to experience High Fashion. We believe in a world where Fashion is not limited by expenditure. We insist on creating more voices in Fashion and seeing the Art people create with our clothing articles

HG Community

Creating voices one style at a time.
HG started out with 2 persons in a library brainstorming High Fashion to over 100 across the country. With people depending on us to provide their style, we care deeply about the work we do. We accept change, learn hard and move with the trends and we seek impact in everything we do.

HG Care

We care and that is why we give back to the enviroment. We understand the harm Fashion does to our wildlife and enviroment.That is why our pieces are faux leather and fur. We would never take the life of an animal just to provide comfort. We care!
